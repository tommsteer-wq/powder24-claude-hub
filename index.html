Powder24 — Claude AI Usage Policy

1. Purpose

Powder24 has provided access to Claude, an AI assistant developed by Anthropic, to help team members work more effectively. This policy exists to ensure we use Claude responsibly, professionally, and in a way that protects both the company and our people.

2. Appropriate Use

  • Claude may be used to assist with writing, research, planning, summarising, brainstorming, and other work-related tasks.
  • Always apply your own professional judgement to any output Claude produces — treat it as a helpful starting point, not a final answer.
  • Claude can make mistakes. Verify important facts, figures, and advice before acting on them.

3. Confidentiality

  • Do not input sensitive personal data (customer details, payment information, private employee records) into Claude.
  • Treat conversations with Claude as you would an external tool — be mindful of what you share.
  • Do not share your Claude login credentials with anyone else.

4. Professional Standards

  • Claude should not be used to produce content that is harmful, misleading, discriminatory, or that could bring Powder24 into disrepute.
  • Do not use Claude to impersonate colleagues, clients, or partners.
  • Any content produced with the help of AI and shared externally should be reviewed and approved as you would any other company communication.

5. Copyright & Intellectual Property

  • Be aware that AI-generated content may not always be fully original. Do not pass off AI-generated content as entirely your own original work in contexts where that would be misleading.
  • Do not use Claude to reproduce large portions of copyrighted material.

6. Accountability

  • You are responsible for how you use Claude. The company retains the right to review usage in line with our standard IT policies.
  • If you're ever unsure whether a particular use is appropriate, check with Tom or Lou before proceeding.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated as AI tools and best practices evolve. You'll be notified of any significant changes via this platform.
